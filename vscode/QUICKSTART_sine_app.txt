Q: Which model should I use?
A: Start with SINE for seasonal analysis. Most weather follows annual cycles.

Q: How much data do I need?
A: Minimum 10 days, recommended 365+ days for sine, 30+ for linear.

Q: What if my model doesn't fit well?
A: Try a different model type, increase data range, or check data quality.

Q: Can I analyze multiple cities?
A: Yes! Run analysis for one city, then change city and run again.

Q: How do I save results?
A: Take screenshots of plots, or copy statistics from details panel.

Q: What does phase mean in sine model?
A: Phase shift indicates when temperature peaks. Adjust for your hemisphere.
